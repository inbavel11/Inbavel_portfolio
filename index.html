The Disaster Damage Assessment Mobile App

The Disaster Damage Assessment Mobile App uses advanced AI and machine learning to evaluate disaster damage in real-time. Users can capture and upload images, which are processed by TensorFlow Lite models for precise segmentation. The app integrates real-time and historical weather data, enhancing context and accuracy. It features a user-friendly interface and employs robust security measures. With optional cloud integration, the app ensures scalability and can handle large-scale disaster data efficiently. Initial tests showed over 90% accuracy in identifying damage, with a 30% improvement in assessment speed compared to traditional methods. This app significantly enhances disaster response by providing rapid and accurate damage assessments

vehicle count in Real Time

A real-time vehicle counting system using YOLOv8 combines advanced object detection and deep learning techniques to accurately monitor and count vehicles in traffic footage. YOLOv8 (You Only Look Once, version 8) is known for its high speed and precision in detecting objects. It processes live video streams from traffic cameras, identifying and classifying different types of vehicles such as cars, trucks, and motorcycles in real time. The system uses deep learning algorithms to track vehicle movements across frames, ensuring accurate counts even in dense traffic conditions. This technology provides essential data for traffic management, urban planning, and safety enhancement, enabling authorities to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and improve road safety with real-time insights. The integration of YOLOv8 ensures efficient and reliable vehicle detection, making it an ideal solution for modern smart city infrastructures.

On duty Management System

The On-Duty Management System is a web-based application developed using PHP and vanilla CSS, designed to streamline the process of managing on-duty requests in educational institutions. Students can create and manage their profiles, submit on-duty requests specifying the date, time, and reason, while staff members can review, accept, or deny these requests. The system maintains a record of all requests and decisions, ensuring efficient and transparent handling of on-duty requests.
